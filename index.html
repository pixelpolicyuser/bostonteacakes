Our Story

A Legacy Baked Into Every Crumb

The Boston Tea Cake Company has been a Worcester institution since 1993. What began as a devotion to quality — real ingredients, real recipes, real care — has grown into a brand that people remember and return to, year after year.

Our gourmet bundt cakes are individually prepared from scratch. No fillers. No shortcuts. Just the kind of baking that takes time, skill, and genuine pride in the craft.
